## 摘要
二次推断函数（QIF）为处理相关数据的边际回归提供了一种稳健且高效的广义估计方程（GEE）替代方法，尤其在整群随机试验（CRTs）中。然而，现有的QIF方法未考虑因治疗组间协变量不平衡导致的混杂，这在观察性CRT或进行预后协变量调整的CRT中常见。我们提出了一种双重稳健QIF（DR-QIF）估计量，它将从倾向得分和结果回归模型构建的双重稳健伪结果与QIF扩展得分方程相结合。当倾向得分模型或结果回归模型中至少有一个正确指定时（不必两者都正确），DR-QIF估计量对平均处理效应的估计是一致的。我们证明，在工作相关结构错误指定的情况下，DR-QIF比双重稳健GEE（DR-GEE）更高效，并解析了渐近效率增益的特征。对于横截面CRT，两种估计量在代数上相同；在具有强时间相关性的纵向CRT中，效率增益显现，在N=120、T=8次重复测量时达到3.5%。通过蒙特卡罗模拟评估有限样本性质，并使用肯尼亚WASH Benefits整群随机试验的数据进行方法说明。

## Abstract
Quadratic inference functions (QIF) provide a robust and efficient alternative to generalized estimating equations (GEE) for marginal regression with correlated data, particularly in cluster randomized trials (CRTs). However, existing QIF methodology does not account for confounding due to covariate imbalance between treatment arms, a common concern in observational CRTs or CRTs with prognostic covariate adjustment. We propose a doubly robust QIF (DR-QIF) estimator that combines doubly robust pseudo-outcomes, constructed from propensity score and outcome regression models, with the QIF extended score equations. The DR-QIF estimator is consistent for the average treatment effect when either the propensity score model or the outcome regression model is correctly specified, but not necessarily both. We show that DR-QIF is more efficient than doubly robust GEE (DR-GEE) when the working correlation structure is misspecified, and we characterize the asymptotic efficiency gain analytically. For cross-sectional CRTs the two estimators are algebraically identical; efficiency gains emerge in longitudinal CRTs with strong temporal correlation, reaching 3.5% at N=120 and T=8 repeated measures. Finite-sample properties are evaluated via Monte Carlo simulation, and the method is illustrated using data from the WASH Benefits Kenya cluster randomized trial.
